About Me

Back to Top

Hello, my name is Tony. Welcome to my Portfolio. Here, you can see some of my best work throughout my career. I am a Graphic Designer that has worked in the mediums of print and web, and I have a total amount of 5 years experience. I graduated from The College of New Jersey with a Bachelor's of Arts in Interactive Multimedia. This essentially is a technology degree focusd on design whether it be for web, print, or apps. Some of the software I am proficient in include Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign , Figma, Premiere Pro, and Camtasia. I am also skilled with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
